About Me

Hello! My name is Addy C. Acro-Santos, and welcome to parapups.us, the personal website of a professional loser dog!

In reality I'm just a self-domesticated chimp with a particular affinity for anthropomorphic animals. In other words: I'm a furry. I'm also an Electrical Engineering major at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas who's spent most of their life within the confines of the Mojave Desert. I am a second-generation Filipino-American, which means I know what My interests include animation, trivia, technology, civics, and art. My pronouns are he/they, altho I do get a kick out of people thinking I'm a girl.

This is where I dump my art and ideas, my own personal corner of the Internet, away from the walled gardens of social media networks. I'll write blogs about whatever tickles my fancy
